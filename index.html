Welcome to

UHaifa
International

Situated on the top of Mount Carmel with breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea in Israel’s most culturally diverse city, UHaifa International offers boundless and inspiring MultiExperiences that will shape your personal journey and change your life.

Your home-away-from-home in Israel, UHaifa International offers a wide variety of affordable undergraduate, language, and Master’s degree programs in an inclusive and sustainable academic, urban, and natural environment.

Seize your once in a lifetime opportunity to meet students from all over the world in an enriching adventure combining academic excellence, cultural exploration, and personal growth. Apply now and join the MultiExperience!

Our Programs

Study Abroad Programs

UHaifa International's Undergraduate Study Abroad programs offer a fascinating Multi-Experience in Israel that our students will never forget! Our English-taught International special track courses and programs are designed to meet diverse academic interests and incorporate fun field trips and extracurricular activities.

Master Degree Programs

Get your Master's in just one year with UHaifa International! Our diverse International Masters Programs are all taught in English combining up-to-date academic theory and research with real-world skills, experiential learning and practical tools for a competitive edge in the job market.

Short Term and Summer Programs

Language Programs

UHaifa International offers language programs all year round. From semester long courses in Hebrew and Arabic, to intensive winter and summer courses, these programs are designed for international students who are looking to immerse themselves in an intensive language acquisition experience. Courses offered from beginners to advanced levels.

The University of Haifa Presents: Dig, Dive and Discover!

The first of its kind to integrate the full scope of archaeological activities on land, at sea and in the lab under one roof, and building on its globally renowned reputation, University of Haifa’s School of Archaeology and Maritime Cultures (SAMC) has recently launched a new international Master’s program, offering an MSc in Archaeological Sciences.

Read more

 

Get your master’s at UHaifa International. Experience Israel. Feel at Home.

Though October 7th has presented one of the most fragile and challenging times for the state of Israel and the Jewish population globally, the University of Haifa stands as a pillar of academic and cultural resilience.  Situated in a prime location on top of beautiful Mount Carmel, with enchanting views of the Mediterranean Sea, the university continues to welcome students from 90 countries worldwide to a once-in-a-lifetime multicultural learning experience in Israel, in the beating heart of Haifa –where cultures and religions coexist peacefully in an unmatched sense of community.

Read more

 

Discovering 3D Judaism, Israel and the Holocaust at the University of Haifa

Now more than ever, the modern State of Israel cannot be understood without grasping the full scope of Jewish history as context. The ancient Israelite kingdoms, two millennia of Diasporic life, the emergence of the Zionist movement, the Holocaust, seventy-five years of developments within the Jewish State and constant geo-political challenges to date  are all essential background to form a comprehensive understanding of what Israel is today.
